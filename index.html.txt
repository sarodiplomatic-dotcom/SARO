We connect public authorities, private companies, and development partners to advance responsible trade, investment, and professional standards across both markets.</p>

<section id="international">
    <h2>International Development</h2>

Our bilateral mandate aligns with global development priorities in governance, private-sector support, climate-smart sectors, digital transformation, and skills development.</p>
</section>

<section id="projects">
